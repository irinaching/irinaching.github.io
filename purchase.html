"Gift for Mom" by Alyona Zhuk

Description

A six-year old boy brings canned food to the grave of his mother, who has stared to death in Ukrainian city of Bucha because of russians. This picture will never leave my mind. So I want the world to see it as well. To know. And to start calling things by their names. It’s genocide. It’s russians’ genocide against Ukrainians.

Artist

Alyona Zhuk is Ukrainian female tattoo artist and illustrator. She and her daughter are currently evacuated from Kyiv region and temporarily based in Konstanz, Germany. Alyona daily produces illustraions inspired by the war in Ukraine.
